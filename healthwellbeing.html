Sustainability Advisory Committee

Good Health and Wellbeing

The Sustainability Advisory Committee is committed to promoting and stimulating good health and wellbeing in our community. We as a committee believe that a healthy and harmless environment is essential for the wellbeing of all individuals.

Our initiatives focus on:

  • End preventable deaths of newborns and children
  • Promoting physical activity
  • Fight communicable diseases
  • Supporting mental health issues and awareness
  • Improving air and water quality

    • Local Challenges

    • Healthcare accessibility in rural areas
    • Mental health service gaps
    • Aging population care needs
    • Health inequality in disadvantaged communities

    Development Process

    1.

    Analyse and Research

    Analyzing existing health resources and any gaps which occur in the community. Researching local health patterns and trends can help local policy makers with the development of the community.>

    2.

    Planning

    Planning can outline the projected goals and objectives which allows key strategies to take place such as resource allocation.

    3.

    Execution

    Execution of health progrmas and activities can nurture a steady development within the community.

    4.

    Evaluation

    Measure outcomes and implement improvemnts for exisiting strategies based on results and findings.

    We work closely with local organizations and experts to develop and implement sustainable strategies that enhance the overall health of our community.
